### Dr. Jack Mostow's RoboTutor Research Group

- Research Objective of RoboTutor project: continuous refinement of key research goals for the Android app, under the guidance of Dr. Jack Mostow
- Research Hypotheses Testing: development of hypotheses and relevant test cases to ensure robust execution of new functionalities - student model PLACEMENT mode and Next Sentence Prediction (NSP)
- Primary Data Analysis: identification of patterns from app's usage reports via Google Apps Script engine
- Project Management Strategies: configuration of automation tasks, workflow alignment, and implementation of database migration techniques
